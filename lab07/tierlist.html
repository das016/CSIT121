Reflection on Lab 7

Lab 07: Table and Form Lab has introduced me to making basic tables and forms. I have learned how to make a tier-list style page structured with an HTML table. In my webpage, I wanted to focus on an HTML table that displayed some of the strongest good guys in the anime "Dragon Ball Z." Overall, I tried to make my table and form accessible in terms of font-styles, font-sizes, and background colors. I also included the alternate attribute to describe my photos in case they do not display to the user. As a major resource to complete this project, I used the book: Teach Yourself Visually HTML and CSS 2nd Edition written by Guy Hart-Davis. Chapter 11 in this book was all about creating forms. I learned how to create a form by using the opening form tag and the closing form tag. In addition, I learned how to create and add Radio Buttons, check boxes, drop down list of options, and command buttons to a form.
